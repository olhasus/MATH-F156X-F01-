WRH Assignments

Written Homework (WRH) is assigned according to the weekly schedule.

Written Homework (WRH) needs to be completed and submitted to Gradescope (usually on Monday) by the due date posted on the weekly schedule until 11:00 PM.

Your work needs to be well presented with a neat, organized writing (see instructions below).

Written Homework Presentation Guidance:
  • Student Name, ID and Assignment all clearly labeled.
  • Each section/ problem number listed.
  • Clear steps or argument leading to the solution.
  • Lots of white space.
  • Final answer boxed.

    • Solutions will be posted here and at the Blackboard site.
